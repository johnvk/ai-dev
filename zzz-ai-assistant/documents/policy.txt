ZZZ INSURANCE - MOTOR VEHICLE POLICY DOCUMENT

SECTION 1 - COVERAGE
This policy covers damage to your vehicle caused by collision, fire, and theft.
Flood damage is covered only if the Premium Flood Protection add-on is active.
The maximum claim amount per incident is $50,000.

SECTION 2 - CLAIMS PROCESS
To file a claim, contact ZZZ Insurance within 48 hours of the incident.
You must provide photos of the damage, a police report for theft claims,
and your policy number. Claims are processed within 10 business days.

SECTION 3 - EXCLUSIONS
This policy does not cover mechanical breakdown or wear and tear.
Damage caused while driving under the influence is not covered.
Intentional damage is excluded from all claims.

SECTION 4 - PREMIUMS
Premiums are due on the 1st of each month.
A 14-day grace period applies before the policy is suspended.
Late payments attract a $25 administration fee.

SECTION 5 - RENEWAL
Policies auto-renew annually unless cancelled 30 days before expiry.
No-claim discounts of 10% apply after each claim-free year.
Maximum no-claim discount is 50% after 5 consecutive claim-free years.
